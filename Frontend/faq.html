Frequently Asked Questions

What is an IPO?

An Initial Public Offering (IPO) is when a company offers its shares to the public for the first time.

How can I invest in an IPO?

You can invest in an IPO through a stockbroker or an online trading platform that offers IPO applications.

What are the risks of investing in an IPO?

IPO investments carry risks such as market volatility, potential losses, and uncertain future performance.
